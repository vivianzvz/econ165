Block 1 Instructions

  • You will be randomly matched into pairs in each round.
  • Each pair consists of an Employer and a Worker.
  • The employer will:
    • Make a non-binding wage offer
    • Write a message to the worker
  • The worker’s task is to add up to 30 pairs of single-digit numbers.

Payoff rules:

  • Employer’s payoff = number of correct sums – wage paid
  • Worker’s payoff = wage received
